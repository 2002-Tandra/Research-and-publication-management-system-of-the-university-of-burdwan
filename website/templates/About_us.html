Our Project

Welcome to the Research and Publication Management System of the University of Burdwan. This project is developed to help faculty members and researchers efficiently manage and showcase their research work, publications, conferences, and projects.

Objective

The objective of this system is to digitize and streamline the research tracking process for universities, making it easier for administrators and scholars to access, submit, and review academic content.

Key Features

  • Faculty login and dashboard
  • Upload and manage research papers and projects
  • View publication records
  • Generate reports and analysis
  • Admin approval system

Technology Used

  • HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript
  • Python (Flask Framework)
  • MongoDB Database

Developed By

This project is developed by MSC students of the University of Burdwan as part of their final semester curriculum.
